OpenMusic

v0.6.2 Cross-Platform Desktop

OpenMusic is a modern, performance-driven offline music player built for the modern desktop. It combines the speed and safety of Rust with the flexibility of React to deliver a seamless listening experience.

Built for Speed

Leveraging Rust's performance for audio processing and file management, ensuring a lag-free experience even with massive libraries.

Modern UI

A clean, Material Design inspired interface that stays out of your way and lets your music take center stage.

Good to know

If you get a warning that the app cannot be opened or is unrecognized by Windows SmartScreen, click "Run anyway" or "More info". This warning is entirely harmless and only shows because the app is currently unverified.
