OpenStocks

OpenStocks is a comprehensive inventory and sales management solution designed specifically for Android. It empowers small to medium-sized businesses with powerful tools to track stock, manage sales, and generate insights.

Inventory Management

Real-time stock tracking with low-stock alerts and detailed item categorization using Room database.

Sales Analytics

Comprehensive sales reporting and visual analytics to help you understand your business performance at a glance.
