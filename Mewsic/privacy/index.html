Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

At Mewsic, your privacy is our priority. Mewsic is built to be a fast, offline-first music player that respects your data and your device.

1. Local Processing & Data Collection

Mewsic is fundamentally designed to operate strictly offline. All audio playback, metadata extraction, parsing, and data processing occur entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, store, track, or transmit any of your personal data, music library information, listening habits, or usage analytics to any external servers.

2. Network Requests

Mewsic will only make network requests to the internet when absolutely necessary and typically only when explicitly initiated by the user. These scenarios include:

  • Fetching the latest release information from GitHub to notify you about app updates.
  • Retrieving missing cover art, lyrics, or stream resolving when explicitly triggered by a user action or through integrated providers.
When using these features, requests are made to their respective third-party services (such as GitHub, Spotify, or public lyric databases), which are governed by their own privacy policies.

3. Third-Party Plugins

Mewsic features a rich Plugin API that allows you to install third-party extensions and integrations. These plugins have network access and can read data within the Mewsic environment. We do not control, verify, or endorse the privacy practices of any third-party plugins. By installing a plugin, you agree to assume all responsibility for how that plugin handles your data.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect new features, changes in the law, or best practices. Any updates will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or Mewsic's data practices, please reach out via the Mewsic GitHub repository.
